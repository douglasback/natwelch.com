Nat Welch

Nat is a Site Reliability Engineer at Google. He has worked at great companies such as iFixit, Punchd and Adobe in the past doing all kinds of programming. At work and while at play, Nat loves to explore the interaction between communication and design using software. When he is not coding, Nat tends to be listening to music and wandering the city looking for a coffee shop to read and write in. Originally from the San Francisco Bay Area, Nat now lives in London.
