Professionally, Nat works as an SRE on Google Compute Engine. Previously he worked at Punchd and iFixit. You can read more about this on his resume.

Nat publishes most of his code and side projects on GitHub. He posts his long form thoughts on Pseudoweb.net, his short form thoughts on Tumble.io, and all of his photography on Flickr.

Some projects he has been working on lately include code, wallpapers, nabdash and tumble.
